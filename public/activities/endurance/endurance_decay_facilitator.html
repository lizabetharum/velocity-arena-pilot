Step 1: Current power (starts at 100, decays over time)
currentPower = 100 − 17 × 0 ÷ 40 = 100
Step 2: Wheel speed (motor power scaled by remaining power)
wheelSpeed = 100 × 35 ÷ 100 = 35
currentPower starts at 100 (full power) regardless of Speed. Speed determines the motor's top speed (motorPower). Endurance determines how fast power drains (decayRate). These are independent — changing Speed does not affect how long the bot survives.
